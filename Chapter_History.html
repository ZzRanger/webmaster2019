Congratulations to Michelle Chen for winning first place in "Essays in Technology" at the 2018 Kansas TSA Conference!
Essays in Technology is a TSA event where participants must write an comprehensive essay over a prompt given on-site that is related to the topic. Prior to the competitions, students are given a general topic and are expected to thoroughly conduct research as preparation for the event.
