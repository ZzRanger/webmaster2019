Chapter History
How It All Began
Olathe North TSA was originally founded in 1998 by Mr. Brittonhart. Though historically small in number, members of Olathe North TSA are immensely driven and enjoyed sucess at both state and nationals. In 2003, Mr. Wagner, the industrial technology teacher at Olathe North, took over as the sponser of Olathe North TSA in when Mr. Brittonhart left, and is the current sponser of the club. Under Mr. Wagner, Olathe North TSA Chapter continues to flourish in national and state competition, as well as promoting outreach activities.
