Computer Courses

Film Classes

History of Film

In this one semester course you will travel back to the beginning of movies and explore the origins of how we got to the movies of today.

Digital Film

This full year course explores the film process from advanced camera angles, editing techniques, and focused effects to creating your own short films for concept, to screenplay, to finished movie.

E-News

In this year-long course, students are a part of the Eagle news team. The team produces several shows each week for our school & community audiences.

Video 1

A one semester introductory course to explore & learn about digital video cameras, special effects, & electronic news gathering skills

Digital Design Classes

Basic Digital Photo

In this course, students will learn how to use a DSLR digital camera and how to manipulate their photos into cool projects. The focus of this course is Photoshop skills with photography included.

Digital Design

In this class, students will learn how to use both Photoshop & Illustrator as a design tool. This is an art class on the computer, so many of the same terms & concepts are used, including how to incorporate the elements & principles of design into your work.

Advanced Digital Photo

This course is for the dedicated digital photographer who has successfully completed the Basic Digital Photo class. This class differs from the basic course, as your photography becomes the main focus, while using programs like Photoshop to alter and improve and edit your work. This course takes considerable time outside of class to complete the photo taking requirements on a weekly basis.

Advanced Digital Design and Advertising

This is an advanced course and student should have successfully completed the beginning digital design course. Students are expected to bring considerable photoshop & Illustrator knowledge to this course. In this class, students will have a variety of real world design projects, such as posters, t-shirts, logos, etc for “real” clients. Students will continue to increase their skills in both Photoshop & Illustrator. This course is for the serious designer that wants to continue where digital design class left off. This is a great class for those needing portfolio pieces for college or job market.
