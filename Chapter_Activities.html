Olathe TSA Conference
The Olathe TSA Conference was held in November of 2018. Half of Olathe North's TSA members participated, with one of our members placing third in Dragster Design.
Kansas State Conference
This year's Kansas State TSA Conference will be held between April 17th - 19th at Webster Conference Center in Salina, Kansas. All 6 members will attend the conference, competing in multiple events and representing the chapter.
Ladies in Tech [LIT]
Members of Olathe North TSA have gone out of their way to create a programming club at Olathe North called Ladies in Tech, tagetting females that may have an interest in the stem field. Every week, the club meets to improve their programming skills, and occasionally guest speakers are brought in to share their knowledge of computer programming
