Competitions
Olathe TSA Conference
The Olathe TSA Conference was held in November of 2018. Half of Olathe North's TSA members participated, with one of our members placing third in Dragster Design.
Kansas State Conference
This year's Kansas State TSA Conference was held during April 17th - 19th at Webster Conference Center in Salina, Kansas. All 7 members attended the conference, placing in multiple events.
National TSA Conference
This year's TSA National Conference will be held between June 28th and July 2nd in Washington D.C. 4 members from Kansas TSA will be present to represent the school and chapter.
Ladies in Tech [LIT]
Members of Olathe North TSA have gone out of their way to create a programming club at Olathe North called Ladies in Tech, targeting females that may have an interest in the STEM field. Every week, the club meets to improve their programming skills, and occasionally, guest speakers are invited to share their knowledge and experience in the STEM field.
